    Bridging the Gap Between Imaging and Data

    Advanced Quantitative Imaging

    This platform goes beyond simple visualization by providing essential tools for radiomic analysis. By incorporating Region of Interest (ROI) metrics and Hounsfield Unit (HU) probing, users can extract objective, quantitative data from medical scans.

    Precision Diagnostics

    Engineered for diagnostic precision, featuring high-fidelity windowing controls and accurate spatial measurement tools. The seamless integration of a calibrated digital ruler allows for exact anatomical measurements in millimeters.

    Precision Imaging for the Future of Medical Research

    Optimized Workflow

    Prioritizing speed and fluidity, the software utilizes hardware-accelerated rendering and intelligent coordinate mapping. Users can perform real-time zooming and panning without latency during clinical reviews.

    Scalable Research Foundation

    Built with a focus on interoperability, this viewer serves as a robust foundation for academic research. The architecture supports complex DICOM metadata handling, making it a viable tool for longitudinal studies.
